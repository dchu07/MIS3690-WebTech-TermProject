MIS3690 Term Project Proposal - Demi Chu

What is your website about?

My website is going to be a travel blog. About the newest and hottest places to travel to, ranging from extravagent places to affordable places to stay at.

What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

The objective of my website is first for myself, to explore all the places I've been or want to go to.

Second, it's for my community, for others to realize amazing new places they've never been or known about.

Third, for the tourism industry. Travel blogging is very helpful to the tourism industry as it is free advertisment for various countries and hotels.

Who is your target audience set?

My target audience ranges from young twenties who want afforadble and fun travel locations, to late thirties to forties who want a more relaxing and luxurious traveling experience.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

Home Page - Which includes a simple side bar to click on different pages

Locations/Destinations Page - Page with a list of locations, so it's easy for others to access and click into which location their interested in

Featured Page - Where all the articles are shown but in the order of recently posted articles

Photos Page - Photos of different location, where people can just click onto the photos they're attracted to, to find the location

Videos Page - Filled with videos of specific locations and hotel videos

About Page - Short description of what the page is about and who it is for

Contact/Feedback Page - Includes how to contact me if there are any questions
